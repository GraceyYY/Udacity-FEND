Koala

Because this eucalypt diet has limited nutritional and caloric content, koalas are largely sedentary and sleep up to 20 hours a day.

  • Scientific Name: Phascolarctos cinereus
  • Average Length: 60-80 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 13-18 years
  • Habitat: open eucalypt woodlands

The koala is an arboreal herbivorous marsupial native to Australia. The koala is found in coastal areas of the mainland's eastern and southern regions input Australia. It is easily recognisable by its stout, tailless body and large head with round, fluffy ears and large, spoon-shaped nose.
